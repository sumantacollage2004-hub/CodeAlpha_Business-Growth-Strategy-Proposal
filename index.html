02

Marketing Mix — 4Ps

A balanced framework that aligns product decisions, pricing strategy, distribution, and promotion into a unified go-to-market approach.

P

Product

  • Core SaaS platform with modular feature add-ons
  • Mobile-first design with offline capability
  • Freemium entry tier to lower acquisition barrier
  • White-label option for B2B enterprise clients
  • Quarterly feature releases driven by NPS data
P

Price

  • Value-based pricing aligned to customer ROI
  • 3-tier model: Starter · Growth · Enterprise
  • Annual subscription discount of 20%
  • Usage-based add-ons for scalability
  • Introductory 30-day free trial, no credit card
P

Place

  • Direct-to-consumer via branded website & app
  • B2B partnerships with industry resellers
  • SaaS marketplace listings (G2, Capterra, AppSumo)
  • API integrations with ecosystem partners
  • Regional expansion via localised landing pages
P

Promotion

  • Content marketing — blog, YouTube, podcast
  • Performance ads on Google & LinkedIn
  • Influencer & affiliate referral programme
  • Monthly webinars and live product demos
  • Community-led growth via Slack & Discord

Long-Term Growth

Years 2–5: Scale operations, expand markets, and build durable competitive moats.

Year 2

Market Expansion

Enter 2 adjacent verticals, launch localised campaigns for APAC & EMEA markets, introduce enterprise-tier with dedicated success managers.

Product Ecosystem

Build open API marketplace, onboard 50+ third-party integration partners, launch developer community and hackathons.

Strategic Acquisitions

Acquire 1–2 complementary micro-startups to accelerate capabilities, consolidate brand under unified platform story.

Market Leadership & Exit

Achieve category leadership in primary vertical, prepare for Series B/IPO readiness, establish thought leadership through proprietary industry research.
